Instructions

Camera Controls

  • Press "o" to enter observation mode
  • Press "t" to enter topview mode
  • Press "r" to enter first person "rat's view"
  • Press "n" and "m" to decrease and increase the render distance.

Movement Controls

  • "q" and "e" rotate the rat
  • "w" and "s" move rate forward and backward
  • "a" and "d" cause the rat to strafe.

World Controls

  • Press "u" and "i" to move the light's x_target west and east.
  • Press "z" and "x" to move the light's y_target south and north.
  • Press "<" and ">" to move the light's z_target down and up.
  • Press "g" and "h" to decrease and increase water wave intensity.

This is a 10,000 x 10,000 world that you can freely explore. If you get too close to the right or upper edge, some wacky bug happens. This is a feature, not a bug...

In Observation/Top view

  • Press "+/=" to zoom in
  • Press "-" to zoom out
  • Hint: holding "=/+" or "-" in observation mode looks cool

In Rat's view

Press space to hide this menu.
